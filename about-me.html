About Me

Hello, my name is Jamodin Maliwanag. My ethnicity is Filipino but I was born and raised in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Unfortunately, I cannot speak Tagalog very well but I’m able to understand a few words. I grew up living with my grandparents at their house, along with my parents. Later on, my parents and I ended up moving quite often, to either houses or apartments. I’m currently living with my parents and our dog Ryder. He’s a purebred Poodle and he’s 2 years old. The reason we named him Ryder is because he enjoys car rides.

My dog Ryder

The reason I’ve enrolled in the Software Development program is because I’ve always had an interest in games ever since I was young. The first time I was introduced to video games was at the age of 5 and it was Pokemon Red for the GameBoy. I’d like to land a job in the game industry working for Riot Games or Ubisoft, but I’d also be fine with any company. I enjoy technology because of the many options it provides. If you don’t know how to get to a friend’s house, you can use Google Maps to provide you a route, along with how long it will take to get there. It’s also really convenient to be able to buy almost anything online from services such as Amazon or UberEats.

Me in Vancouver

One thing I’m most passionate about is gaming. Since I started playing games at a very young age, I’ve grown attached to them. There are many genres and types of games I’ve experienced and I’ve enjoyed most of what I played. Currently, my favourite games are Apex Legends and Osu. Some other hobbies I have are trying to learn instruments. Right now I’m practising the melodica and I would like to buy a piano to practise with as well. I still like to go biking from time to time and while I don't anymore, I enjoyed playing tennis and volleyball.
